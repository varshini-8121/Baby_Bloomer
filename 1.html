!!Not the right age for pregnancy!!



Adolescence is a critical period of growth and development, impacting educational pursuits, career aspirations, and personal development. Teenage bodies may not be fully prepared for pregnancy, increasing the risk of complications such as preterm birth and low birth weight. Pregnant adolescents may face social and emotional challenges, including stigma, isolation, and financial strain. Comprehensive sex education, access to contraception, and supportive resources are crucial for informed decision-making. Seek immediate medical care and counseling for support and guidance. Explore resources tailored to young mothers, such as educational programs and support groups. Make informed decisions about the pregnancy considering personal readiness and future goals. This condensed version retains the essential information while providing a more concise overview of the considerations for pregnant adolescents.
